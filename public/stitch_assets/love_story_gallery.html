The Beginning

A Journey of
Two Souls Intertwined

It started with a shared glance across a crowded room in Hyderabad, a moment frozen in time that neither of us could have predicted would lead here. Dev, with his quiet strength and Sithara, with her radiant joy, found a harmony that felt as ancient as the stars.

From the bustling streets of India to the serene landscapes of our travels together, every step has been a chapter in a story written with laughter, deep conversations, and an unwavering belief in one another. Ours is not just a wedding; it's a celebration of a friendship that evolved into the greatest love we've ever known.
